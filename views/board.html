How To Play

Move your muons (or anti-muons) from your initial quadrant to form a triangle in any one of the other three pyramid quadrants. The first player to create a triangle of 3 muons inside one of the quadrants, wins the game. Once you have cleared all 5 of your muons out of your initial quadrant, you can win by forming a trangle in your home quadrant aswell.
